Here provides Input Voltage Sensitivity and ECO Outlet two advanced features to control UPS. The feature definitions are description as below.

Input Voltage Sensitivity

Users can set up the adequate sensitivity according to the equipment and the power. Once the utility power is out of range by the UPS, the UPS will switch to battery mode to provide the power to the connected equipment immediately.

The sensitivity options are explained below Medium sensitivity is the default to the utility power. The low-quality power from the fuel generator may cause the UPS to switch the battery mode frequently. In order to avoid this condition,

Low sensitivity is recommended. If the equipment required a higher quality of power, High sensitivity is recommended but may result in the frequent discharging.

Shutdown type

This setting will specify the manner in which computer is shutdown. The options are Shutdown or Hibernation. The Hibernation option is only visible on operating systems and hardware that support hibernation. When the PowerPanel Personal shuts down the hosted computer using a Shutdown, any unnamed files will be saved automatically in a folder named Auto Saved in the My Documents folder. After the System is shutdown, the UPS will get a signal to shutdown itself. Once Power is restored UPS will Reboot automatically. If in the BIOS of your Computer the automatic startup by power restore is activated the Computer will restart by itself.
