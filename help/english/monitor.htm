Current Status and Summary provide details and a summary of the status of the UPS and historical events.

  • Current Status
    Displays details of the UPS and explains whether the status of the UPS is normal or abnormal.

  • Summary
    Displays power events and relevant figures such as the number of times, and length of time of each historical power event. It also displays the total cumulative value of power events.

  • Event Logs
    Displays the total number of events and records details of the historical events.
