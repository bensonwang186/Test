  3. Low Runtime Remaining
  • In line mode

    The PowerPanel® will signal a warning of low runtime remaining when the UPS can only supply 5 minutes or less of battery power. In order to eliminate this warning you can move some equipment from UPS outlets to surge protected outlets.

  • In battery mode

    When battery power remains less 2 minutes, the computer will warn to initiate a shutdown or hibernate. Your computer may shut down unexpectedly after 2 minutes if you ignore this warning.

*Note : power problem means a condition which causes the UPS to use battery power, such as power outage, over voltage or under voltage.
Note : This is not available for all UPS models.
