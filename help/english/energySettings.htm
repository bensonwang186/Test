On Energy Settings page, users can set the average rate of power consumption and carbon emission by selecting the country, or assign and apply a customized rate. When the rates are updated, the information displayed on the energy reporting page will also be updated.

Country Selection

Power costs vary by countries, and therefore the Energy Cost and equivalent carbon emission are different. By selecting the country, users can set up the average rate of power consumption and carbon emission.

  • Country
    Select the country where the UPS is located.

Energy Cost

Since the rate of power consumption can vary over time, users can assign rates for different periods of time.

  • CO2 Emissions
    Since the rate of carbon emissions also changes over time, users can update the carbon emission per kWh.

  • CO2 Emitted per kWh
    The weight of equivalent emitted carbon when one kWh power is consumed.

  • Unit of Measurement
    Select the unit of measurement in kilogram or pound.
